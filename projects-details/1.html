Waterwell constructions

Issue:

Many families in Uganda move long distances to access clean drinking water. We can have these Waterwells made for the price = €700 each. It will solve the problem of waterborne diseases.

What we can do:

We can construct them a waterwell We want to focus on the villages all along Eastern Uganda where the women are very poor and use dirty water from ponds and swamps to cook and clean. While we do this we can teach them how to overcome waterborne diseases.

Cost:

for deep Waterwells = we will use €700

p>for shallow Waterwells = we will use €500

What we have done:

we have covered over 200+ villages so far in Mbale city area. And now we want to focus on the villages in the whole of Eastern Uganda.

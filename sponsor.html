Orphan Sponsorship

Issue:

There are a few orphanages in Uganda. The issue is lack of sponsoring. Also the issue is sponsors do not contiunue, due to life or financial issues.

What we can do:

we want to take a sponsorship for an orphan for a year and put them into an orphanage and pay their year fees in 1 go. After this we can also allow you to check on the orphans progress in health and quran and in well being.

Cost:

€500 for 1 year (their food daily, shelter, and study fees in an orphanage)

What we have done:

we helped so far more than 200 orphans into orphanages. We are currently sponsoring following orphanages.
